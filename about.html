Awards

Sotavento has been recognized as the first hotel in the world to be awarded by the EarthCheck’s Sustainable Design Gold Certification. The award reflects the resort unwavering commitment to sustainability, by making positive changes to reduce the environmental footprint and improve social impact.

EARTH CHECK is the world’s leading environmental benchmarking and certification programme designed specifically for the travel and tourism industry, having over 1,500 clients in 70 countries. The EarthCheck programme is built on Agenda 21 principles, monitoring projects in key areas such as sustainability approach, energy, water and waste planning, social, cultural and economic wellbeing, and innovation.
